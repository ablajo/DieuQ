Welcome to DieuQ

DieuQ is a community of creators and operators backing entrepreneurs in building scalable businesses for Ghana and its Diaspora.

About Our Company

We are a community of creators and operators backing entrepreneurs in building scalable businesses for GHana and its Diaspora.
we invest in founders and teams with expertise, technical know-how and have attained some traction.

Members

Interested in backing early stage African tech startups
Interested in learning about early stage angel investments
Looking to diversify their investment portfolio and put their savings to work
Interested in working for and/or with early stage and growth startups

Founders

we invest in founders building companies that meet the following criteria:

Early stage tech startups with African founders
Companies with a technical co-founder, team member and/or cheap technical costs
Revenue generating start-ups with traction
Pre-seed, seed or series A participation only.

Our Value Proposition

for the founders we provide:
for our members we provide:
Funding to allow them to move faster and achieve desirable growth
Access to early stage startup investment opportunities
Expertise and know-how by leveraging the knowledge and experience of our members and extended network
Enablement as an investor- ROI typically better than putting monet with a financial institution
Human capital to companies we have invested in, when they start to scale
Sense of purpose by putting your funds towards companies doing meaningful work

How It Works

1

Members get a shortlist of deals after engaging with startup founders

2

Members review deals and show commitment by completing a form

3

Capital Call is made where commitment is sufficient to proceed

4

Investment Committee completes Enhanced Due Diligence to close

5

Investing members get period updates from portfolio companies
